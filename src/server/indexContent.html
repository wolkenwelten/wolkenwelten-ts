How To Play

Jump into the action with these simple controls:

Keyboard/Mouse

  • WASD - Move around the islands
  • Space - Jump (double jump for extra air time!)
  • Left Click - Light attack (fast but less knockback)
  • Shift - Dash (brief moment of invulnerability)

Gamepad

  • Left Stick - Move around the islands
  • Right Stick - Look around
  • A - Jump (double jump for extra air time!)
  • B - Light attack (fast but less knockback)
  • X - Dash (brief moment of invulnerability)

Touch (Tablet/Smartphone)

  • Just touch the screen and use the on-screen controls
